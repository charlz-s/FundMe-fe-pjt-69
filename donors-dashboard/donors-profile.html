About Misturah Adebayo

Misturah Adebayo

Misturah Adebayo is the individual Giving Manager at Provide. Misturah manages the identification, outreach, and stewardship of Provider's major donors and strategic plan for annual giving donors, all with the goal of developing support to forward Provide's important work.

Prior to working at Provide Misturah was part of the development teams at the University of Lagose and Museum of Arts. She received her Master of Arts Management from Yaba College of Technology and Bachelor of Arts Management from Pennsylvania State University. She currently lives in her hometown of Ikeja, PA with her husband, dog, and beautiful new baby boy. When she is not at work, Misturah enjoys cooking, gardening, playing, music, and hanging out with friends and her amazing family.
